Why This Matters
Unmanaged MCI is a significant and addressable cost driver in your 65+ population.

Patients who progress from MCI to Alzheimer’s Disease incur substantially higher annual costs than stable MCI patients. Early identification and routing to targeted Individual Care Plans is the most actionable intervention available — and Acurist is the only platform that makes it scalable across a Medicare Advantage or commercial population.
