The Business Case
For every 50,000 members, a 15% MCI prevalence rate means 7,500 members at risk — and a $75M+ cost exposure from unmanaged progression.

Published claims data shows that patients who progress from MCI to Alzheimer's Disease incur $10,058 more per year in total medical costs than stable MCI patients. Early identification and routing to targeted Individual Care Plans is the most actuarially defensible intervention available — and Acurist is the only platform that makes it scalable.
