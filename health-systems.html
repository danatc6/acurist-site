The V28 Opportunity

Why V28 makes Acurist essential now.

CMS V28 materially increased the need for accurate cognitive risk identification, staging, and coding. The transition reduces overall Medicare Advantage risk scores by an average of 3.12% — but V28 also expanded dementia into mild, moderate, and severe HCC categories (125–127), each at a 0.341 RAF value. Health systems that can detect and stage cognitive impairment accurately protect against RADV clawbacks and capture recurring RAF revenue; those that can’t leak both.
