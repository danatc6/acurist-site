How to Get Started

Your clinic is up and running in 4 steps.

The entire setup process takes under 15 minutes. Any staff member can administer the test — no specialist credentials required.

1

Create your free account at MCI Screen

Visit mciscreen.com and click “Free 15-Day Trial” or the “Get Started” tab. Complete the short registration form — your staff can complete it on the practice’s behalf.

⚠️ Important: enter your Acurist representative’s name in the “Where did you hear about us?” field
Go to mciscreen.com/get-started →
2

Set up user accounts for your team

Each staff member who will administer the MCI Screen should have their own user account. This ensures tight control over patient data access. All accounts within the practice can be “grouped” so every user can see the practice’s full screening history.

3

Train your staff (10 minutes)

Watch the MCI Screen Demonstration Video with Q&A below. No specific credentials or clinical background required — good patient interaction skills are the only requirement. Most staff are ready to administer after a single viewing.

4

Begin screening patients

The MCI Screen is fully guided and runs online — no software to download. Administer from any computer, tablet, or phone. The report generates instantly and is ready before the physician enters the room.

📅 After your 15-day trial, assessments are billed at $79 per assessment via credit card or invoice
