How to Get Started

Four steps to your first MCI Screen

Create your account at mciscreen.com

Visit the MCI Screen website and click “Free 15 Day Trial” or the “Get Started” tab to begin. Fill out the registration form — it’s self-explanatory and takes under 5 minutes.

Go to mciscreen.com →
2

Set up individual user accounts for each staff member

Each test administrator in your practice should have their own separate user account. This ensures tight control over access to patient data and enables longitudinal tracking of patient assessments.

No special credentials required. Anyone with good patient interaction skills can administer the MCI Screen. Group all accounts within your practice so every user can view and manage patient records across the clinic.
3

Train your staff with the demonstration video

Watch the “MCI Screen Demonstration Video with Q&A” below with your team before your first screening. The video covers administration, scoring, and how to interpret the report with patients.

Tip: The Implementation Guide PDF walks through every step in detail. Download it and keep a copy at the front desk for reference during your first few screenings.
Watch the training video ↓ Download Implementation Guide →
4

Run your first assessment — and get a real-time report

The MCI Screen is a fully guided, 10-minute word recall assessment administered online. No downloads required. Access from any internet-enabled computer, tablet, or handheld device.

Results are generated instantly — with the patient’s Overall Impression (Normal / Below Normal), their Memory Performance Index (MPI) score on a scale of 0–100, item scores for each cognitive task, and a clinical discussion based on peer-reviewed literature.

After 15 days: A message will appear notifying you that Acurist.AI will charge $79 per assessment. You’ll be asked to accept the terms to continue access. Payment via credit card or invoice to your office address.

Account Structure

Built for how your clinic actually works

The MCI Screen platform is designed for busy clinical practices. Every staff member gets their own login. Every patient record is securely grouped and accessible across your whole team.

🔒 Secure Online Access

All data exchange and storage complies with HIPAA guidelines, brokered through SSL transactions. No downloads or local software required. Access your account and all patient records from any internet-enabled computer, tablet, or handheld device — at any time, from anywhere.

👤

Individual User Accounts

Each test administrator in your practice gets their own separate account. This ensures tight control over patient data access and creates a clear audit trail for every assessment performed.

🏠

Practice Grouping

All accounts within your practice can be “grouped” so every user can view and manage patient records across the clinic. Enables seamless management of patient flow from any device.

📊

Longitudinal Tracking

All past assessment reports are accessible through easy pull-down menus. Compare results over time, track changes in MPI scores, and view, print, or save reports as PDFs at any time.

⚡

No IT Burden

Because the MCI Screen runs entirely online with no local downloads, your practice never has to worry about system maintenance, product updates, or data backups.
